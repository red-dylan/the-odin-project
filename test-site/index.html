Maisie Puppy

Maisie is a puppy dog. She is a Golden Retreiver - Cocker Spaniel Mix who was rescued from South Carolina using Petfinder.

Maisie is 10 months old and loves to play. She loves:

  • tennis balls
  • chewing on bones
  • barking at rabbits
  • cuddling

Maisie goes on walks everyday. Most days she walks to the dog park to play with her friends.
